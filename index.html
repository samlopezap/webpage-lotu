About LOTU Systems

LOTU Systems represents the convergence of exceptional talent, innovative thinking, and strategic vision in the aerospace industry. Our team leverages the rich pool of engineering expertise across Latin America, connecting regional capabilities with global aerospace demands.

We are committed to developing sustainable aerospace solutions that not only meet today's challenges but anticipate tomorrow's needs. Through our extensive network and collaborative approach, we deliver cutting-edge systems engineering, design, and analysis services to clients worldwide.

Our mission extends beyond engineering excellence—we aim to establish Latin America as a key player in the global aerospace ecosystem, fostering innovation and creating opportunities for the next generation of aerospace professionals.

Our Services

Systems Engineering / MBSE

Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) approaches to design, analyze, and optimize complex aerospace systems throughout their lifecycle.

  • Requirements analysis and management
  • System architecture design
  • Verification and validation
  • Lifecycle management

CAD Design

Advanced computer-aided design services for aerospace components and systems, from conceptual sketches to detailed manufacturing drawings.

  • 3D modeling and visualization
  • Parametric design optimization
  • Technical documentation
  • Manufacturing support

CFD & FEA Analysis

Computational Fluid Dynamics and Finite Element Analysis to predict and optimize the performance of aerospace structures and fluid systems.

  • Aerodynamic analysis
  • Structural analysis
  • Thermal analysis
  • Multiphysics simulations

Software Development

Custom software solutions for aerospace applications, including simulation tools, data analysis platforms, and automation systems.

  • Simulation software
  • Data analysis tools
  • Automation systems
  • Integration solutions
